🏢 Change Management Business Processes

✅ Approval Workflows

Structured decision making:

  • Change Advisory Board (CAB) review
  • Security team assessment
  • Business stakeholder sign-off
  • Emergency change procedures

👤 Ownership and Accountability

Clear responsibility assignment:

  • Change sponsor (business owner)
  • Change implementer (technical owner)
  • Change reviewer (quality assurance)
  • Change manager (process oversight)

👥 Stakeholder Engagement

Comprehensive involvement:

  • Business users (requirements)
  • IT operations (implementation)
  • Security teams (risk assessment)
  • Compliance officers (regulatory review)

📊 Impact Assessment

Comprehensive risk evaluation:

  • Security implications analysis
  • System performance impact
  • Compliance requirement effects
  • Business continuity considerations
