📜 Compliance Framework Categories

🏛️ Regulatory Compliance Requirements
Legal mandate adherence:
• GDPR: EU data protection regulation
• HIPAA: Healthcare information security
• SOX: Financial reporting controls
• PCI DSS: Payment card data security
• FISMA: Federal information security
🏆 Industry Standards and Frameworks
Voluntary best practice adoption:
• ISO 27001: Information security management
• NIST CSF: Cybersecurity framework
• CIS Controls: Critical security controls
• COBIT: IT governance framework
• ITIL: IT service management
📊 Compliance Monitoring and Assessment
Continuous adherence verification:
• Automated compliance scanning
• Manual assessment procedures
• Audit trail maintenance
• Gap analysis and remediation
• Compliance dashboard reporting
📋 Documentation and Reporting
Evidence and communication management:
• Compliance evidence collection
• Audit preparation and support
• Certification maintenance
• Executive reporting and summaries
• Regulatory submission preparation

🌍 Key Global Regulatory Frameworks

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): European Union privacy law with global reach and impact
  • HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act): US healthcare data protection requirements
  • SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act): US financial reporting and internal control requirements
  • PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard): Credit card data protection requirements
  • FISMA (Federal Information Security Management Act): US federal agency security requirements
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act): California state privacy protection law
  • NIST Framework: Cybersecurity framework for critical infrastructure protection
