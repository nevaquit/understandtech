User and Role-Based Training

Untrained users represent a serious vulnerability because they are susceptible to social engineering and malware attacks and may be careless when handling sensitive or confidential data. Appropriate security awareness training needs to be delivered to employees at all levels, including end users, technical staff, and executives. Training should be tailored to the audience and job role
