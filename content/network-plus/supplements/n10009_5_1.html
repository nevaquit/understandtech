Exam focus

Explain the troubleshooting methodology

  • Identify the Problem Gather info; question users; identify symptoms; determine what changed; duplicate the problem
  • Establish a Theory of Probable Cause Question the obvious; consider OSI model (top-down, bottom-up, divide and conquer)
  • Test the Theory Confirm or deny the theory; if denied, establish a new theory or escalate
  • Document Everything Record findings, actions, outcomes, and lessons learned
  • Scenario questions will describe a technician mid-process and ask "What should the technician do NEXT?" You must identify the current step and know what follows. Step 7 (Documentation) is ALWAYS the last step — never skip it.
