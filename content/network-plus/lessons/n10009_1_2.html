Pro tip

Forward Proxy vs. Reverse Proxy: A forward proxy sits in front of clients and intercepts outbound requests (used for content filtering, anonymity). A reverse proxy sits in front of servers and intercepts inbound requests (used for load balancing, SSL termination). Forward proxy protects clients; reverse proxy protects servers.
