Exam objective N10-009 5.1

Explain the troubleshooting methodology

This methodology is the backbone of Domain 5.0 and will appear in scenario-based questions throughout the exam.

StepActionKey activities
1Identify the ProblemGather info; question users; identify symptoms; determine what changed; duplicate the problem
2Establish a Theory of Probable CauseQuestion the obvious; consider OSI model (top-down, bottom-up, divide and conquer)
3Test the TheoryConfirm or deny the theory; if denied, establish a new theory or escalate
4Establish a Plan of ActionDetermine fix; identify potential side effects; plan rollback
5Implement the Solution or EscalateApply the fix; if unable, escalate to appropriate team
6Verify Full System FunctionalityTest all affected systems; implement preventive measures
7Document EverythingRecord findings, actions, outcomes, and lessons learned

Exam focus

Explain the troubleshooting methodology

  • Follow all seven steps in order; documentation is always last.
  • “What should they do NEXT?” questions test step sequence.
  • Establish theory before making changes; plan rollback.
  • Escalate when scope or access exceeds your role.

Scenario discipline
