Explain important factors of physical installations

  • MDF (Main Distribution Frame): The central wiring hub for a building or campus; connects to the ISP and houses core networking equipment.
  • IDF (Intermediate Distribution Frame): Secondary wiring closets that connect to the MDF and serve a floor or section of a building.
  • UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply): Provides battery backup to protect against power outages and surges.
  • PDU (Power Distribution Unit): Distributes power to multiple devices within a rack; may include monitoring capabilities.
  • Environmental: Maintain 64-80°F (18-27°C) and 45-55% relative humidity. Use clean agent fire suppression systems (FM-200) that do not damage equipment.
