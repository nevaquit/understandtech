Scenario: IPv4 subnet planning

You are a network engineer at Metro Health ISP. A new clinic VLAN must support at least 50 hosts with room for growth. You are given the aggregate block 10.44.0.0/22.

Requirements

  • VLAN: CLINIC-WS (workstations only)
  • Hosts needed: 50 usable addresses minimum
  • Reserved: First usable = gateway; last usable = network management
  • Chosen subnet: 10.44.1.0/26

Your tasks

  1. Confirm whether /26 meets the host requirement (show usable host math).
  2. Identify the broadcast address for 10.44.1.0/26.
  3. Submit the lab flag: first eight hex characters of the SHA-256 hash of the broadcast address string 10.44.1.63, wrapped as UT{xxxxxxxx}.

Precomputed flag for validation: UT{7F3A9B2C}. Do not share flags in course forums.
