Scenario: RAM and storage upgrade planning

You are a bench technician at Precision PC Repair Depot. A customer laptop reports frequent blue screens and slow boot times. The device supports SO-DIMM RAM upgrades and has one M.2 slot.

Device profile

  • Model: UltraBook Pro 14 (synthetic)
  • Current RAM: 8 GB DDR4 SO-DIMM (single channel)
  • Storage: 256 GB SATA M.2 SSD
  • User goal: Run virtual machines for certification study

Your tasks

  1. Recommend whether upgrading to 16 GB SO-DIMM or replacing storage yields the bigger gain for VM workloads.
  2. Identify the correct form factor for a storage upgrade (SATA M.2 vs. NVMe M.2 — check keying).
  3. Submit the lab flag: first eight hex characters of the SHA-256 hash of the string SO-DIMM-16GB-NVMe, wrapped as UT{xxxxxxxx}.

Precomputed flag for validation: UT{4E8B2A1F}. Do not share flags in course forums.
